Hamburger

Description

A standard but yummi hamburger. A classic

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Mayo
  • Beef Patty
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Cut bun.
  2. Spread mayo on bun.
  3. Put previously fried patty.
  4. Put cheese on the patty.
  5. Add lettuce and tomato.
  6. Add ketchup and mustard.
