Tacos al Pastor

Description

These rock. Also, pineapple.

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Al Pastor Pork
  • Chopped Onion
  • Chopped Coriander
  • Grilled Pineapple
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Heat tortillas.
  2. Grill pork and put it on the tortillas.
  3. Add onion, coriander and pineapple.
  4. Liberally add salsa.
  5. Also lime, if you're animal.
