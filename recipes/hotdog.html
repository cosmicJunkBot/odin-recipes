Hot Dog

Description

Bun. Sausage. Nothing that basic should be so yummi.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Mayo
  • Steamed Sausage
  • Chopped Onion
  • Chopped Tomato
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Heat bun.
  2. Spread mayo on one of the insides of the bun.
  3. Place sausage.
  4. Put chopped onion and tomato.
  5. Liberally add ketchup and mustard.
