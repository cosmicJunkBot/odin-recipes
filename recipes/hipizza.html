Hawaiian Pizza

Description

A delicious pizza with a tropical touch.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Canadian Bacon
  • Pineapple

Steps

  1. Spread dough.
  2. Spread sauce on the dough.
  3. Liberally toss mozzarella.
  4. Put the canadian bacon and the pineapple on top of the mozzarella.
  5. Bake in the oven.
